What is K-pop About

K-Pop is a genre of music that has hit America by storm. If you can't tell with K-pop is by the "K", K-Pop is korean pop music. Now I know a lot of people are already turned off of the idea of korean music, but you have to give it a couple chances. I first found K-pop in 2013 and at first I didn't like it, but then I kept on getting notifications for it, and one of them caught my attention, and I clicked on the video. That video was what made me start to like k-pop. After that I looked up some more groups and I listened to there music. Just like any other artist in america, every person/group has diffrent sounding music. From there I have found two groups that I really like. The groups are EXO and BTS. Both are really good bands, but there songs are diffrent. Now what normally attracts someone to K-pop music is the beat of the music, most beats in kpop are very upbeat and lively. Another thing is the choreography in there music videos. Many people find the dances very powerful and energetic. Those are most of the reasons why I like Kpop. the Livley music, the energetic dancing, but I also like the poersonalities of the groups.Now for people that want to know more about the groups I like, just scroll down.

Like I said before the groups I listen to are EXO and BTS there are others but these two I listen to a lot more than others. EXO is a 9 member band, they used to have 12 but three left do to how the company treated them. EXO has made a lot of albums and the all have a lot of different sounding songs on each album. Some are sad, some are happy. some are quieter and others are really loud.That is one of the things I ike about EXO. BTS is a 7 member group, and their music is very energetic. That is what I love about BTS (They are my favorite band), They have very upbeat songs that have really good meaing to it.
